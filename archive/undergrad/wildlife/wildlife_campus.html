On Campus

The wildlife society brings speakers, demonstrators and documentaries to campus.

A Life Among Whales

In 2006 the Wildlife Society brought the documentary A Life Among Whales in MUB theaters. This documentary follows biologist Roger Payne through beautiful underwater photography as he describes all the obstacles to whales continued survival, including whaling and ocean pollution.

Coalition to Restore the Eastern Wolf

This year several members of the Wildlife Society contacted the Coalition to Restore the Eastern Wolf and attended educational meetings to learn more about them. A speaker from the group came to UNH to speak to the Wildlife Society and describe their project.
